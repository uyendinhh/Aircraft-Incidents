Aircraft Incidents Safety Guide

This visualization tries to inform any concerned passengers about fatal incidents over time, per airplane model, and per phase of flight.

Incidents Over Time and Space

Drag the slider to see a choropleth map of the world, coded by fatal incidents in the selected year.

Airplane Model Statistics

These star plots are color coded by manufacturer. Statistics are computed based on grouping by manufacturer as an attempt to keep a consistent scale. Note, certain models are simply more popular than others leading to higher statistics.

Phases of Flight

Drag the slider to see the number of fatalities in each phase of flight. Note, many incidents have been excluded due to being uncategorized. If included, the majority of fatalities would be within the uncategorized group.
